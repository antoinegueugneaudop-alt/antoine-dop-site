Antoine Gueugneau is a French Director of Photography working across series, feature films, documentaries and international productions.

His images are shaped by light, framing, texture and atmosphere, with precise attention to preparation, production constraints and narrative intent.

His visual preparation work extends this cinematographer’s eye into preproduction, helping directors and producers explore atmosphere, framing, light, time-of-day and live-action integration before the shoot.
